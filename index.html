United: A Global Aviation Leader

United Airlines continues to be a titan in the aviation industry, currently operating one of the largest domestic and international flight networks in the world. As a founding member of the Star Alliance, United offers passengers unparalleled connectivity to over 1,300 destinations globally. In recent years, the airline has embarked on its "United Next" ambitious growth plan, which involves retrofitting its entire mainline fleet with upgraded interiors, larger overhead bins, and seatback entertainment at every seat, aiming to close the gap between the budget and premium flying experiences.

United: MileagePlus Program

In terms of perks, the United MileagePlus program remains a favorite for frequent travelers due to the fact that miles never expire and can be used on a vast array of partner airlines. For those looking for a more elevated experience, the United Polaris business class offers some of the best international lounges and lie-flat bedding in the sky. Furthermore, United has been making headlines for its commitment to sustainability, being one of the first major carriers to invest heavily in Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) and vertical takeoff electric "air taxis" to reduce their carbon footprint in the coming decades.
